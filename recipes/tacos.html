Chicken tacos

This is a popular choice in tacos as well. Chicken is marinated and then cut into small pieces, making it the perfect filling for your tacos. Some will also prepare it by shredding it before putting in the tortillas.

Ingredients

    Steps

  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite size pieces
  • 1 cup lemonade
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 ½ teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 (12 ounce) package corn tortillas
  • 2 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 head lettuce, shredded
  • 1 (8 ounce) package shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
  • 1 (8 ounce) jar salsa
  • 1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
